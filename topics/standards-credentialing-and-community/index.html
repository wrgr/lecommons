Advancing Workforce Learning through Learning Engineering

IEEE ICICLE — Invitation to LE Webinar Series · 2025

On October 23rd, 2025, the ICICLE Workforce Market Interest Group (MIG) led a webinar on Advancing Workforce Learning through Learning Engineering. The MIG co-chairs, DJ Jaeger and John Costa, explored how learning engineering supports workforce transformation through data-informed design, system-level thinking, and evidence-based practices that link learning to performance outcomes.

Webinar Expert conversation Invitation to LE High-Consequence & Complex DomainsStandards, Credentialing & Community

Applying Learning Engineering in a Global Context

IEEE ICICLE — Invitation to LE Webinar Series · 2026

An Invitation of Learning Engineering held on February 25, 2026, explored how learning engineering is adapted globally in a local context. Hosted by the Globalisation Special Interest Group, Carolina Ali Fojaco, Chief Learning Officer at Project Alianza, provided a deep dive on how they used learning engineering to iterate and scale AnGo, an offline, AI-assisted literacy app to help children in Central America build foundational reading skills.

Crazy Idea: What If We Use Engineering to Develop and Deliver Engineering Education?

Abul Azad, Jim Goodell · IFEES / GEDC / IUCEE · 2025

Goodell in conversation with Azad (IFEES) on the provocative inverse of our normal framing: if LE is how we rigorously teach people to engineer, why don't we use LE to teach engineering itself? A 2025 entry worth watching for the international-engineering-education perspective.
